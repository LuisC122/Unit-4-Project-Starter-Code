About Me

I was born 8 November 1966, and I'm a British chef, restaurateur, television personality and writer. My restaurant group, Gordon Ramsay Restaurants, was founded in 1997 and has been awarded 17 Michelin stars overall; it currently holds a total of seven. My signature restaurant, Restaurant Gordon Ramsay in Chelsea, London, has held three Michelin stars since 2001. After rising to fame on the British television miniseries Boiling Point in 1999, I've become one of the best-known and most influential chefs in the World.
